About Us

Peak POS Pro is a dynamic company at the intersection of financial technology and electronics repair. Established in 2022, our mission is to provide seamless point-of-sale (POS) solutions to businesses of all sizes, while also offering expert repair services for a wide range of electronic devices. We aim to enhance operational efficiency and ensure that technology remains an enabler, not a barrier, for our clients. We have successfully delivered electronic payment solutions in Central and East Africa, with ambition to expand to the Greater Africa region. The combination of cutting edge, robust and secure software with on-the-ground client and retailer payment network management, in creating transformative payment solutions in Africa. Peak POS Pro has already began the process of ISO and PCI certification of the repair centre which should be completed in third quarter of 2026.
