Other Peripherals

Repair Process

Initial analysis involves in-depth diagnostics. Software reloads and configuration are done to resolve simple functional issues. Where applicable, we prepare for the next level of repair.

We replace hardware components such as worn out keypads, screens, sim boards, antennas, printers, and other swappable parts.

This level of service covers intricate repairs, involving the replacement of sophisticated components like Integrated Circuits (ICs), diodes, USB ports, CMOS batteries, and card readers.

Before any delivery, we conduct rigorous Quality Assurance to meticulously verify everything against the agreed-upon Service Level Agreement (SLA).
